ABOUT DESSERT PALACE

Our Founders

Our foundsers, Soren Calluma and Ezran Baits met in Tokyo International Cooking University in 1985, and aggreed that they both wanted to pursue the art of dessert making and shared it with the world. However, it was not until 1990 that this dream came true, when a local businessman tasted their strawberry mousse and decided to invest in their business.

The Rise to Popularity

Despite gathering the funds to build their dream cafe, success was not within reach. However when Dessert Palace managed to make it onto the Tokyo Culinary magazine, its rise to popularity began, and business was booming!

Our Philosophy

Despite garnering success, we do not believe that it is enough to just achieve fame. What we wish to do is also inspire others from around the world to enter the world of dessert making. Click on #CHLG to learn more.
